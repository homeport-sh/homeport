## Why binaries

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Nothing on the server">The box runs SSH and Caddy; your app is one executable behind a health check.</Card>
	<Card title="Atomic deploys">Upload beside the old release, health-check, flip a symlink. Rollback is flipping it back.</Card>
	<Card title="Hardened by default">Bootstrap sets up key-only SSH, no root login, a firewall, fail2ban, auto security updates.</Card>
	<Card title="Your keys, your box">Secrets travel over ssh stdin, never argv or git. A leaked CI key is scoped to one app.</Card>
</CardGrid>
